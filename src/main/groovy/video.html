Project Video

To wrap up my project, I thought that it would be interesting to try and determine if people really cared about cleaning up pollution. The way I decided that I would prove this was to set a water bottle right beside a trash can. This would prove if anybody cares enough about the environment to take five seconds out of their "busy" day to pick up a simple water bottle. My hypothesis was that nobody would take the time to pick up the water bottle, nor even give it a second glance. The video is here:

The purpose of this video was to demonstrate society's indifference to trash that, in the long run, could cause a major problem. Many people walked past the bottle; however, nobody picked it up. In fact at one point, a person stood right next to the water bottle and instead of throwing it away, they kicked it. I feel that this experiment does a good job of proving my point that most people show apathy for cleaning up the environment, even if it is a minor inconvenience for them.
